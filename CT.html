Once a patient has successfully received charity care, the ACT and referrals teams will notify the Head TS of the week, confirm that the referral is still necessary, and schedule the patient's appointment. For urgent referrals, Dr. Meah will draft a request to Radiology Associates with the EC.

Access to Care Team


Please click here when you arrive at your patient's FS appointment for further directions.

Once your patient has charity care, please contact the CT Referrals Manager. The CT referrals manager will give you directions on how to schedule your patient for their specialty referral appointment.

Cost

Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their income, Financial Services will grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1, the CT scan w/o contrast will cost $15. To find out the costs for patients who are not Level 1, please contact the CT scans Manager, Chiaki Nakata

Cost to Society: CT scans w/o contrast cost ~500 in new york out of pocket.

Wait time

How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services. One average this takes 24 days Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 1 week for a ct scan.
