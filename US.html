Senior Clinician

Teaching Senior

Someone from the referrals team will schedule the ultrasound for your patient and follow up with her.

Access to Care Team

When calling IMA, make sure to have: the patient’s name, MRN, specific ultrasound test, and the next time they will be seen at EHHOP. Ideally, the appointment should be made before the patient’s next visit to EHHOP. Make sure to ask for specific instructions. Generally, those are:

  • U/S Abdomen: cannot eat or drink after midnight of night before
  • U/S Pelvic complete: Drink 4 cups of water one half-hour before exam (the patient may instead be asked to come in an hour early to drink water)

Unless otherwise mentioned, ultrasounds take place in CAM building (same building as EHHOP), 17 E 102nd St, at end of hallway on sixth floor.

Cost
